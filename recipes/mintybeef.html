Minty Beef Fudge

Description

Hello my future sandwich eater. This is what I look like. I'm fudge, prepared in a microwave. Please P.M. me if I'm on Yahoochat. Bye. Thanks for stopping by.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup hamburger crumbles*
  • 1 12oz pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tps vanilla
  • 1 cup white deluxe baking pieces
  • 1 drop red or green food coloring
*Hamburger crumbles are made by frying off a pound of hamburger with 1/4 cup water; stirring constantly to break up the meat. Place on paper towel to drain off excess liquid and cool. Remaining crumbles can be frozen for future use.

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate chips with 1 cup milk in microwave. Cook only until mixture starts to bubble; stir until smooth
  2. Add vanilla
  3. Spread Half of mixture into a waxed paper-lined 8" square pan
  4. Chill in freezer about 10 minutes. Hold remaining mixture at room temperature
  5. Microwave remaining milk with white chocolate baking chips
  6. Add extract, and food coloring, if desired
  7. Spread on chilled chocolate layer
  8. Chill about 10 minutes in freezer
  9. Add beef crumbles to reserved chocolate and spread on top
  10. Score into small pieces and chill for 2 hours
  11. Bon Appetite!!!
