Sand Hamwich

Description

Hello my future sandwich eater. This is what I look like. I'm bread in between ham, on a cheap thriftstore plate. Please P.M. me if I'm on Yahoochat. Bye. Thanks for stopping by.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices ham
  • 1 slice bread
  • 6 tbsp regret

Steps

  1. Take ham and put on plate. Spread ham nicely to make it seem welcome, friendly, and opening
  2. Place bread in between ham and close the package
  3. Seal their fates with a stake* driven through the heart
  4. Bon Appetite!!!
*Note: a toothpick may suffice if no stakes are available
